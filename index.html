A Masterpiece of the 9th Century

Prambanan stands as one of Southeast Asia's greatest architectural achievements. Built in the 9th century, this UNESCO World Heritage Site showcases intricate stone carvings and legendary stories that shaped Javanese culture.

What Makes Prambanan Special?

Points:

  • Ancient Hindu architecture
  • The legend of Rara Jonggrang
  • Majestic 47-meter main temple
  • Famous Ramayana Ballet performance
